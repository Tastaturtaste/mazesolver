Make sure swig, python and mingw/g++ are installed and in the PATH.

In cmd:
1. swig -python -c++ PathFinder2_swig.i
	output: PathFinder2_swig_wrap.cxx | pathfinder.py


2. g++ -c PathFinder2_swig_wrap.cxx PathFinder2_swig.cpp -I"Path/to/python/include" -D_hypot=hypot
	output: PathFinder2_swig_wrap.o | PathFinder2_swig.o

-D_hypot=hypot is a define-statement necessary, because of a nameclash between python.h and cmath.h


3. g++ -shared PathFinder2_swig_wrap.o PathFinder2_swig.o -o _pathfinder.pyd -L"Path/to/python/libs" -l"pythonxx.lib"
	output: _pathfinder.pyd
